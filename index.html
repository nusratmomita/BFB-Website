1 Million Diasporas Raising 1 Billion
for 1 Trillion Economic Impact - Let’s
Make it Real

In October, 2024, shortly after the revolution in Bangladesh, a small group of Bangladeshis from the diaspora came together and developed this BHAG (Big Hairy Audacious Goal) to raise $1 billion USD to invest in Bangladesh’s economy to support the Three Zero initiative as envisioned in the book “A World of Three Zeros” by Dr. Yunus.

Billions For Bangladesh (BFB) is quickly taking shape as it embarks on this very ambitious but worthwhile journey. The Company will identify and enlist multiple Board of Directors including the 5 co-founders from the global diaspora.

To unite the global Bangladeshi diaspora to drive transformative change in Bangladesh through innovative investments, impactful initiatives, and collective expertise, fostering sustainable development for generations to come.

We will offer our services unconditionally for Bangladesh & Bangladeshi Citizens without any hidden agenda. If you are excited about the possibilities in the same way that we are, come and join the initiative.

To build a globally connected Bangladeshi community that catalyzes $1 billion in impactful development annually, transforming Bangladesh into a model of prosperity, inclusivity, and sustainability.

Billions For Bangladesh is an unprecedented and groundbreaking initiative uniting millions of Bangladeshi diaspora members worldwide to raise $1 billion. We aim to fund transformative projects across education, healthcare, renewable energy, agriculture.

Trust – We take the responsibility of managing The Fund with integrity and to the best of our abilities. We respect the trust of our investors, commit to upholding the trust of the citizens of Bangladesh and the stakeholders of the diaspora.

Transparency & Accountability – We will be transparent on how the funds are being invested and ensure proper accountability of those investments. We will keep our digital dashboard updated for the diaspora to stay informed.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is BFB?
BFB (Bangladeshis For Bangladesh) is a platform that connects the Bangladeshi diaspora with opportunities to contribute to development projects in Bangladesh. It enables Bangladeshis living abroad to directly support initiatives that drive positive change in their homeland.
2. Who can contribute to BFB?
Anyone, anywhere. If you're a Bangladeshi living abroad, you can contribute financially or by spreading the word. BFB is open to individuals whose roots belong to Bangladesh and want to contribute to his/her motherland. 
3. How does BFB ensure that funds are used effectively?
BFB implements strict transparency measures including detailed project tracking, regular financial reports, and third-party audits. All funded projects undergo thorough vetting, and we provide regular updates on project milestones and outcomes to ensure accountability.
4. What sectors does BFB support?
BFB supports diverse development sectors including education, healthcare, climate resilience, technology, infrastructure, and economic empowerment. Our focus is on projects that create sustainable, long-term impact for communities across Bangladesh.
5. Can I track the impact of my contributions?
Yes, BFB provides comprehensive impact reporting for all projects. Contributors receive regular updates on project progress, outcomes, and real-world impact. Our digital platform allows you to track exactly how your contributions are making a difference.
6. Is my donation tax-deductible?
Tax deductibility depends on your country of residence. BFB is registered as a non-profit organization in several countries, and many contributors can claim tax benefits. We recommend consulting with your tax advisor regarding specific deductions available in your jurisdiction.
7. Is BFB a government initiative?
No, BFB is an independent non-governmental organization. While we collaborate with government agencies on certain projects, we operate independently to ensure flexibility and effectiveness in our approach to development work.
8. How can I get involved beyond donating?
There are many ways to contribute beyond financial donations. You can volunteer your skills, become an ambassador in your community, help with awareness campaigns, provide professional expertise, or even propose and lead new projects that align with BFB's mission.
9. How is BFB different from other crowdfunding platforms?
BFB is specifically designed for the Bangladeshi diaspora with a focus on long-term national development rather than individual fundraising. We offer specialized knowledge of Bangladesh's needs, vetted projects with measurable outcomes, and a community of like-minded contributors committed to Bangladesh's future.
10. Why should the diaspora trust BFB?
BFB builds trust through complete transparency, rigorous project selection, regular reporting, and third-party verification. Our leadership team includes respected professionals from the diaspora, and we maintain the highest standards of governance and accountability in all our operations.
