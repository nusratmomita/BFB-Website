Innovating and Collaborating for a Bangladesh that Endures Prosperity

01

One Billion Strong: Bringing Change through Grassroots
Movement

We’re on a bold mission to raise $1 billion in contributions to transform Bangladesh’s key
development sectors. The first step was launching a digital platform that acts as a powerful
communication and engagement hub connecting changemakers, visionaries, and contributors
who believe in shaping a stronger future for Bangladesh.

02

One Million Voices: United to Collaborate on One Vision

We will be transparent on how the funds are being invested and ensure proper accountability of
those investments. We will keep our digital dashboard updated for the diaspora to stay
informed.

03

From Insight to Impact: Co-Creating the Future of Bangladesh

Seasoned professionals, thought leaders, and innovators will join forces with visionary minds to
co-create solutions that can drive real change. With a powerful blend of financial support and
expert knowledge, this united front will help design and deliver transformative solutions for
Bangladesh’s most pressing challenges.

1 Million Diasporas Raising 1 Billion
for 1 Trillion Economic Impact - Let’s
Make it Real

In October, 2024, shortly after the revolution in Bangladesh, a small group of Bangladeshis from the diaspora came together and developed this BHAG (Big Hairy Audacious Goal) to raise $1 billion USD to invest in Bangladesh’s economy to support the Three Zero initiative as envisioned in the book “A World of Three Zeros” by Dr. Yunus.

Billions For Bangladesh (BFB) is quickly taking shape as it embarks on this very ambitious but worthwhile journey. The Company will identify and enlist multiple Board of Directors including the 5 co-founders from the global diaspora.
